Meerkat Reader is a fast, friendly RSS reader for Android. It is based on Capy Reader and continues as an independently evolved app with a new name, visual identity, and feature set.

Some features include:

• Syncing via Feedbin, FreshRSS, Miniflux, Google Reader API-compatible services, or directly to your device
• Track read and starred articles
• Save articles for later
• Article search
• Full content extraction
• Article image caching and reader preloading
• Backup and restore for subscriptions and settings
• Headlines widget
• Notifications
• Dark mode
• Big screen support
• Import and export your saved feeds
